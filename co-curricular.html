Co-Curricular Activities

Encouraging holistic growth: in and outside the classroom

At St. Patrick Hills School, we believe education is more than just textbooks and tests. While traditional classroom learning lays the foundation for academic growth, it often falls short in nurturing the whole child. That’s where our vibrant co-curricular program steps in — bridging the gap between theory and real-world readiness. From fostering moral values and social skills to unlocking creativity and building physical and emotional strength, our co-curricular activities create a dynamic space where students grow into well-rounded, confident individuals. It’s not just school — it’s a launchpad for life.

Camp Site

We are proud to be the only school in East Africa with a dedicated, purpose-built campsite designed exclusively for our students’ relaxation and rejuvenation. Spanning 35 acres, this serene haven lies just a short distance from our main campus, perched on the southern slopes of the Ngong Hills and overlooking the majestic cliffs of the Great Rift Valley. The panoramic views are nothing short of breathtaking—an inspiring, tranquil escape from the rigours of academic life. Here, students can hike scenic trails, explore freely, and immerse themselves in one of Kenya’s most beautiful and calming landscapes. It is a true paradise—an environment where minds can unwind, spirits can soar, and nature becomes the ultimate classroom for rest and reflection.

Music and Drama

At St. Patrick’s Hill Schools, music and drama are gateways to self-discovery, confidence, and creative expression. We nurture each student’s unique voice through performance, encouraging bold ideas, collaboration, and imaginative thinking. Whether mastering an instrument or performing on stage, students grow in confidence and empathy—skills that extend far beyond the classroom. Here, we don’t just teach music and drama—we empower students to take center stage in their own stories.

Educational Trips

At St. Patrick’s Hill Schools, we believe the world is our classroom. Since 1996, our students have explored far beyond the school gates—traveling across Kenya and to destinations like Tanzania, Egypt, South Africa, the UK, UAE, USA, Sweden, Mauritius, and more. These journeys open minds, spark curiosity, and build global awareness in a world that’s more connected than ever. One parent put it best: “I chose St. Patrick’s because the local and international trips have helped my children grow both broadly and deeply.” We couldn’t agree more. Travel at St. Patrick’s isn’t just about seeing new places—it’s about expanding horizons, building confidence, and shaping global citizens.

Sports

At St. Patrick’s Hill Schools, sports aren’t just an activity—they’re a vibrant part of our students’ growth and well-being. We believe in the power of play to rejuvenate the mind, energize the body, and build character. Our expansive recreation park is a haven for movement and joy, equipped with everything from athletics tracks and basketball courts to tennis, netball, volleyball, handball, and even rounders. Younger children thrive in our dedicated play park, while students of all ages dive into fun at our ultra-modern swimming pool complex.

Clubs

At St. Patrick’s Senior School, learning doesn’t end when the bell rings—it takes on new life through our vibrant clubs and societies. Here, curiosity is contagious and every student has a chance to discover what excites them beyond the classroom. Whether it’s experimenting in the Science Club, capturing moments in Photography, expressing faith in Christian Union, or sparring in Karate, there’s a space for every passion to thrive. From debating big ideas to exploring the wild, reporting campus news, creating music, or championing peace, our clubs empower students to lead, create, question, and connect. Girls and boys alike find community, build confidence, and uncover talents that shape who they become. At St. Patrick’s, clubs aren’t just activities—they’re launchpads for future innovators, artists, and changemakers.
