Who We Are

About Us

St. Patrick’s Hill Schools is a Christian co-educational boarding and day school established with the aim of fostering every student’s moral, intellectual, social, aesthetic and physical development. The school was founded over two decades ago on the vision of providing an all-round quality learning to pupils in a relaxed and serene environment that assures every child of maximum benefit in positive character growth and academic excellence.

Mission

To foster and nurture a complete pupil in moral and aesthetic values underpinned upon a strong belief in God’s guidance; to inculcate in every learner the value of sound discipline in all aspects of school life and beyond; to help every pupil aspire to attain the highest possible achievement in all facets of learning the better to become a worthy citizen of this world.

Vision

To create a learning institution of choice that is the dream and envy of every learner in terms of quality professional service, facilities and environment.

Testimonials

What parents and alumni say about us

Mr. Laswai

Parent

St. Patrick’s Hill Schools holds a special place in my heart. For nearly twenty years, I’ve been a proud parent here, with all my children passing through its nurturing halls. That alone speaks volumes. I’ve watched them grow—emotionally, physically, and academically—thanks to the school’s warm environment, dedicated staff, and firm foundation of discipline. It’s this blend of care and structure that has shaped them into the respectful, grounded young adults they are today.

Dr. Wairimu Monica Waweru

Alumnus (Class of '04)

February 5th, 2002 marked the start of a life-changing journey—the opening of St. Patrick’s Senior School. I joined as one of the first Form 2 students, struggling with poor grades and little hope for the future. But Mr. and Mrs. Kang’ethe saw potential in me, and everything changed. Discipline, respect, faith, and all-rounded growth were at the heart of our learning. With just five students, we received unwavering support, which helped me rise from a D to an A. Today, I’m a doctor because of the foundation St. Patrick’s gave me. This school on the hill will always be my home.

Linet Wanjiru

Alumnus (Class of '24)

St. Patrick’s wasn’t just my school—it became my family. From quiet Form 1 days to confidently leading debates and scoring top grades, I’ve grown in every way. The teachers believed in me, even when I doubted myself, and that made all the difference. I’m walking into the future with courage, faith, and discipline—just as St. Patrick’s taught me. Forever proud to be a Patrician!
