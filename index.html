Who We Are

About Us

St. Patrick’s Hill Schools is a Christian co-educational boarding and day school established with the aim of fostering every student’s moral, intellectual, social, aesthetic and physical development. The school was founded over two decades ago on the vision of providing an all-round quality learning to pupils in a relaxed and serene environment that assures every child of maximum benefit in positive character growth and academic excellence.

Mission

To foster and nurture a complete pupil in moral and aesthetic values underpinned upon a strong belief in God’s guidance; to inculcate in every learner the value of sound discipline in all aspects of school life and beyond; to help every pupil aspire to attain the highest possible achievement in all facets of learning the better to become a worthy citizen of this world.

Vision

To create a learning institution of choice that is the dream and envy of every learner in terms of quality professional service, facilities and environment.
