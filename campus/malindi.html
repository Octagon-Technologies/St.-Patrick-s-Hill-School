Opened in 2011, St. Patrick’s Hill School, Malindi is a vibrant preparatory school offering a nurturing education from Kindergarten to Standard 8. Rooted in the same values of discipline and excellence as our Kiserian campus, Malindi has quickly earned a strong reputation in the community for academic success, outstanding facilities, and a joyful learning environment. Here, children grow in confidence and character—guided by caring, qualified teachers from the age of three all the way to K.C.P.E. Whether day or boarding, every learner benefits from a balanced education that combines gentle discipline, academic achievement, and enriching recreation. Now, the exceptional St. Patrick’s experience is right at your doorstep in Pwani—bringing excellence closer to home.

St. Patrick’s Preparatory School is where the journey begins. As the heart of the St. Patrick’s Hill Schools, it has nurtured young minds for over 20 years—built firmly on Christian values, discipline, and a commitment to excellence. Our dedicated teachers support each child as they take their first steps in learning, equipping them with essential skills, confidence, and curiosity that will carry them through to high school and beyond. With small classes averaging 15 pupils, we provide personalized attention in a nurturing environment where children are encouraged to think independently and grow boldly. Life at the Preparatory School goes beyond academics. Our vibrant extracurricular program—including sports, music, and drama—promotes creativity, teamwork, and a balanced lifestyle. Faith is central to our identity. Weekly chapel services and pastoral care help pupils grow spiritually as part of a caring Christian community. Come and see how we build confident learners and compassionate leaders from the very start.

Founded in 2011, St. Patrick’s Hill School, Malindi is a thriving Christian preparatory school dedicated to nurturing well-rounded, disciplined, and joyful learners from Grade 1 to Grade 8. We lay a strong foundation for academic success, spiritual growth, and personal development through a warm and structured environment. With small class sizes, our experienced teachers provide personalized attention, helping each child build confidence, discover their strengths, and cultivate a love for learning. From the classroom to the playground, we foster curiosity, creativity, and character. Set in a peaceful coastal location, the school combines academic excellence with outstanding facilities, a caring Christian ethos, and opportunities for meaningful recreation. Whether enrolled as a day or boarding student, every child receives the support they need to flourish. St. Patrick’s Malindi is more than a school—it’s a home for growth, discovery, and lifelong learning.
