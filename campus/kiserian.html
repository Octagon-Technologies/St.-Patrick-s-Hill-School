Our Kiserian Campus

A suburbian feel to excellence

  • Fees Structure

Nestled in the peaceful outskirts of Kajiado County, just 20 kilometers from Nairobi, St. Patrick’s Hills offers more than just education — we offer a transformative journey. Our location along the newly tarmacked Pipeline–Isinya Road gives you easy access to a school where learning thrives away from city distractions. At St. Patrick’s Hills, we believe in nurturing more than just academic success. Our progressive curriculum is thoughtfully structured to support growth at every stage — intellectually, socially, and emotionally. Each child is seen, heard, and guided as a unique individual. We place a strong emphasis on real relationships — between teachers and students, among peers, and with parents — because we know learning flourishes in a supportive, connected environment. Our classrooms extend beyond four walls, encouraging curiosity, creativity, and character development through every experience. Come walk through our gates, meet the heart of our school — our students and staff — and see firsthand how we inspire confident, capable learners ready to make a difference.

St. Patrick’s Preparatory School is where the journey begins. As the heart of the St. Patrick’s Hill Schools, it has nurtured young minds for over 20 years—built firmly on Christian values, discipline, and a commitment to excellence. Our dedicated teachers support each child as they take their first steps in learning, equipping them with essential skills, confidence, and curiosity that will carry them through to high school and beyond. With small classes averaging 15 pupils, we provide personalized attention in a nurturing environment where children are encouraged to think independently and grow boldly. Life at the Preparatory School goes beyond academics. Our vibrant extracurricular program—including sports, music, and drama—promotes creativity, teamwork, and a balanced lifestyle. Faith is central to our identity. Weekly chapel services and pastoral care help pupils grow spiritually as part of a caring Christian community. Come and see how we build confident learners and compassionate leaders from the very start.

Founded in 2002, St. Patrick’s Senior Boys’ School has grown into a thriving Christian institution, dedicated to shaping morally grounded, intellectually sharp, and well-rounded young men. We believe in bringing out every boy’s competitive best—nurturing their academic, spiritual, physical, and social development. Our experienced staff give each student individual attention, guiding them to discover their strengths, sharpen their skills, and thrive both inside and outside the classroom. With small class sizes (no more than 15 students), each learner benefits from focused teaching and meaningful mentorship. Our facilities include a well-stocked library and remedial support programs to ensure no student is left behind. We provide a disciplined yet warm environment that fosters independence, leadership, and integrity. Boys leave St. Patrick’s confident, capable, and ready to contribute positively to their communities. Simply put, this is more than a school—it’s a brotherhood, a home, and a launchpad for life.

At St. Patrick’s Senior Girls, we understand that the transition to Senior School is a crucial milestone. From the very first steps in Form One, our expert and caring staff nurture each girl’s confidence, intellect, and character—preparing her to become a poised, articulate young woman ready for the world. With small class sizes averaging 15 students, our learning environment is personal, participatory, and empowering. We encourage curiosity, critical thinking, and self-directed study, while helping students balance academics with personal passions. Beyond the classroom, our vibrant extracurricular program—including sports, music, and debate—builds teamwork, discipline, and leadership. We expect commitment and excellence, supported by a strong pastoral care and counseling system. Since 2009, St. Patrick’s has provided a holistic education—spiritually, intellectually, and physically—shaping well-rounded women of purpose.

At our IGCSE campus, academic rigor meets vibrant student life, strong discipline, and a supportive Christian community. For over a decade, we’ve empowered both girls and boys to thrive—intellectually, morally, and socially. Our modern facilities, passionate teachers, and wide-ranging extracurricular programs create an environment where students are challenged to grow and inspired to lead. We’re proud to shape confident, independent learners who are ready to seize opportunities and make a meaningful impact in the world. Every student is known, valued, and guided with care. Our strong pastoral support system ensures that each child grows in faith, character, and purpose. We welcome students based on academic potential—regardless of background, location, or faith—because excellence knows no bounds.
